关于高中奥数的一些有用链接
Useful Links for High School Mathematical Olympiad

网站
Websites

  • Official Website of IMO
    这是IMO的官方网站. 你可以在这里下载到IMO预选题的题目与解答. 同样, 这里可以找到IMO的新闻与比赛结果.
    This is the official website for International Mathematical Olympiad. Here you can download the IMO shorlist problems and solutions. Also, you can find the news and results of IMO.

  • Art of Problem Solving Online
    这是关于奥数的最著名的在线论坛. 你可以在这里找到来自全世界各地的数学竞赛的题目.
    This is the most famous online forum for mathematical olympiad. You can find the problems from mathematical competitions all over the world.

  • IMO Math
    这个网站上有一些非常好的奥数训练材料.
    This website contains some excellent training materials for IMO.

  • IMO Geometry
    这个网站由 Takis Chronopoulos 建立, 主要关注奥数中的平面几何问题. 值得一提的是, 在 Olympiad problems + solutions 栏目中可以找到许多奥数问题的解答(不止几何).
    This website is created by Takis Chronopoulos, it mainly focuses on plain geometry problems in math olympiad. It is worth mentioning that in the Olympiad problems + solutions section, you can find solutions of many math olympiad problems (not only for plain geometry).

  • 数学新星网
    这个网站由冷岗松教授建立, 是国内最著名的奥数网站. 这里有丰富的资料可以下载.
    This website is created by Gangsong Leng, it is the most famous website for math olympiad in China. You can download a lot of materials here (all materials are in Chinese).
