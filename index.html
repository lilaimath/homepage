About Me

Li (力) is my given name and Lai (赖) is my family name. I am an associate professor at Xiamen University's School of Mathematical Sciences. I am interested in odd zeta values \(\zeta(2k+1)\), \(p\)-adic odd zeta values \(\zeta_p(2k+1)\) and multiple zeta values \(\zeta(k_1,k_2,\ldots,k_r)\). Here is my CV.
